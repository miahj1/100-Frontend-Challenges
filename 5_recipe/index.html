Greek Kofta Kebabs

Total Time
25 minutes
Level
Intermediate
Budget
Under $30

Delicious Greek-style lamb kofta kebab using a secret spice blend from a famous restaurant.

Sauce not included.

Ingredients

  • 500g/ 1 lb lamb mince
  • 3 large garlic cloves
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp mustard powder
  • 4 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 1/2 tsp curry powder
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Combine meat, herbs, spices.
  2. Shape into 5" logs, skewer, flatten to 0.5".
  3. Grill or pan-fry until browned.
  4. Serve in pita with lettuce, tomato, onion, and sauce.
